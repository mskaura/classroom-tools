🌎 Land Use Decisions

How do humans use the land — and what are the consequences? Explore four different land use choices and see what happens to the environment.

Choose a land use approach and observe the environmental consequences:

🌳🌳🌿🌳🌳
🌿
Farming
Convert land to intensive crop farming for food and profit.
🏭
Urban Development
Build housing, roads, and commercial districts on undeveloped land.
🌿️
Conservation
Protect land as a nature reserve or wildlife corridor.
🏞
Indigenous Stewardship
Return land to Indigenous management using traditional practices.

💡 Reflection Questions

Grade 7 — Geography
Interactions in the Physical Environment
B3.3 — Human Impact on Natural Systems
Analyse the effects of various human activities on the natural environment in Canada and explain the concept of sustainability in this context.
B2.4 — Land Use and Resource Management
Explain how land use decisions by governments, corporations, and communities affect both people and the natural environment, with consideration for Indigenous land management practices.
How this tool addresses it: Students make land use decisions and immediately see visual and textual consequences, connecting choices to environmental and social outcomes. The reflection prompts extend thinking beyond the simulation, encouraging higher-order reasoning about sustainability, equity, and Indigenous land relationships.
