Grade 7 & 8 — Social Studies / History
Heritage and Identity: First Nations, Metis, and Inuit Peoples
B2.3 — Treaties and Relationships with Indigenous Peoples
Explain the significance of treaties between First Nations and the Crown, and describe their relevance to people in Canada today.
A1.1 — Indigenous Perspectives & Contributions
Describe the perspectives and contributions of Indigenous groups in shaping Canadian identity, land relationships, and governance structures.
How this tool addresses it: Students interact with a colour-coded schematic map of Canada's major treaty regions, discovering nation names, treaty terms, years signed, and historical context for each area. The five-question quiz assesses comprehension. Respectful framing and emphasis on ongoing land relationships directly supports TRC Calls to Action for K-12 education.
